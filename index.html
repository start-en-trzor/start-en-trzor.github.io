Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up my Trezor hardware wallet?

To set up your Trezor hardware wallet, go to Trezor.io/start and follow the instructions provided for Version 22.9.3.

What is the Trezor Suite?

The Trezor Suite is a software application designed to help you manage your Trezor hardware wallet efficiently and securely.

Can I use my Trezor wallet with multiple devices?

Yes, you can use your Trezor wallet with multiple devices as long as you follow the security protocols provided in the Trezor Suite.

What should I do if I forget my Trezor PIN?

If you forget your Trezor PIN, you will need to use your recovery seed to restore access to your wallet.

Is my Trezor hardware wallet secure?

Yes, Trezor hardware wallets are designed with high-security features to protect your cryptocurrencies from unauthorized access.
